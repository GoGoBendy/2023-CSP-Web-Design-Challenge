(Militarism, Alliances, Imperialism and Nationalism)

Militarism

Militarism is a belief or system where the military is exalted
and it's needs and considerations are given excessive priority.

Alliances

Alliance, in international relations, a formal agreement between
two or more states for mutual support in case of war.

Imperialism

Imperialism is the practice of maintainingan empire, or a
colection of colonies
